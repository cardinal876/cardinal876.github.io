Raysbook is an encyclopedia by the world's leading experts, created so everyone everywhere can learn directly from the people at the forefront of their respective fields.

Also in many high impact research areas, there are only a few people globally who follow or contribute to the latest developments. A goal of Raysbook is to increase awareness, interest, and participation in research in all critical areas. Whether in a university, industry, or a garage, research produces the knowledge that is the foundation for education, economic development, and an ever improving future.
