Join team 10,000! Raysbook creates an encyclopedia with the top 10,000 education-related topics.

Author requirements

A. Doctorate degree in a related field such as a PhD, MD, or JD.

B. Article Length: 150 to 2000 words

C. Article Level: Between 9th grade high school level and college freshman 101 non-major level.

Compensation for Authors:

1. Raysbook sources brand sponsors who post their names, logos, and slogans (not ads because they can be slow to load and distracting) with the encyclopedia articles.

2. Raysbook pays authors 51% of revenue from sponsors (and any other article-related revenue such as licensing to AI-models). However it doesn't pay the authors cash directly so please continue reading to learn the full compensation system.

3. Raysbook pools all the authors' sponsorship revenue into a single venture capital and angel investor fund. Each individual author's share is deteremined by the following formula.

- 2/3 of all revenue is divided equally among authors by length of article. 2/3 of total revenue * page length of article/ total length of all articles.

- 1/3 of all revenue is divided by page views. 1/3 of total revenue * author's page views /total page views.

4. Raysbook hires a venture capitalist or angel investor, with a history of successful investing, to invest the fund into a startup that is growing and has high potential.

5. For the venture capital or angel investment, the authors receive a percentage of the total value of the investment. Each author's percentage is determined by the value of his or her total revenue, described in #3, divided by the total size of the pool.

6. For the final payout of the investment, the authors receive:

100% of the initial value of the investment pool with inflation adjustment.

For any growth beyond the initial investment size and inflation adjustment:

- The venture capitalist or angel investor receives 20% (standard)

- Raysbook receives 29%.

- Author receives 51%.

For example, if the author's initial revenue share was $1000, the startup was aquired in three years and had grown to 10x its initial value at the time of investment,

- Author receives $1090 (100% of initial investment and inflation adjustment) + 4500 = $5590.

- Venture capitalist receives $1800.

- Raysbook receives $2600.

C. Entrepreneurs benefit from receiving investment capital when a growing and high potential opportunity arises.
